About Medbee Diagnostics

Medbee Diagnostics is a modern, fully-equipped pathology laboratory committed to providing accurate and timely diagnostic services. Established with the goal of supporting better patient outcomes, our laboratory combines high-quality instruments with skilled personnel to ensure precision in every test result.

We follow strict quality assurance protocols and use automated analyzers for hematology, biochemistry, microbiology and serology. Our team of experienced pathologists reviews critical results and provides expert guidance to treating physicians when required. Patient safety and confidentiality are top priorities in all our processes.

To make healthcare accessible, we offer home sample collection across our service areas, safe sample transport, and fast online report delivery. Our turnaround times are optimized using efficient logistics and round-the-clock laboratory workflows for emergency testing.

Medbee Diagnostics is also focused on continuous improvement — we regularly calibrate equipment, participate in external quality assessments, and train staff on the latest laboratory best practices. This commitment to quality helps us maintain trust among doctors and patients alike.

Our corporate office in Patna serves as the coordination hub for operations across Bihar & Jharkhand. We are dedicated to expanding our services responsibly while maintaining the accuracy and reliability that our patients depend on.
